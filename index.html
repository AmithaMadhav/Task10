STRIKE PIRAHNA

SCORE : 0

Mario’s Escape from the Piranha Plants

Mario is on a thrilling adventure, running through a dangerous pipe-filled world! The tricky Piranha Plants are waiting to snatch him with their sharp teeth and cunning little flowers. Your mission is simple: protect Mario by striking the Piranha Plants before they catch him. Each time you successfully strike a Piranha Plant, you earn 10 points. But be careful! If you accidentally strike Mario, it’s game over! How long can you keep Mario safe? Test your reflexes and save him from the snapping plants!
